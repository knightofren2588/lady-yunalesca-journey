The Eternal Summoner

Once the first summoner to defeat Sin in the realm of Spira, Lady Yunalesca has transcended death itself. Having sacrificed her mortal form to become an eternal guardian of Zanarkand's sacred dome, she now finds herself drawn to the mystical energies of Azeroth. In this new world, she walks the path of shadow magic, finding familiar echoes of the forbidden arts she once knew.

Her eternal wisdom guides her through Azeroth's trials, comparing each experience to her memories of Spira's endless spiral of death and rebirth. As a Shadow Priest, she embraces the void's whispers, recognizing in them the same cosmic truths that once allowed her to commune with Sin itself.
